Hello!

I’m a UX designer with a lifelong passion for artistic expression, storytelling, and solving meaningful problems. Ever since I was a kid—crayon in hand—I’ve been fascinated by color, form, and the way design shapes how we interact with the world. That creative curiosity led me to study Fine Arts and eventually earn a degree in Information Design.

Over the past 11+ years, I’ve focused on turning complex challenges into intuitive, user-centered solutions. My process combines UX design, research, and front-end development to build experiences that are thoughtful, functional, and human. From mobile apps to large-scale enterprise tools, I love working closely with teams to co-create, iterate, and deliver work that makes an impact.

Outside of design, I’m often exploring new places, sipping on strong coffee, or diving into deep conversations. As a mom of two amazing kids, I get to see the world through their eyes—something that constantly inspires me to stay curious, empathetic, and open to new ideas.

Let’s connect—I'd love to collaborate and bring meaningful experiences to life together.
